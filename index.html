Collecting and Using Your Data

Information We Collect

Usage Data

Quick News does not require any user authentication; therefore, no personal accounts or sign-ups are needed to use the app.

However, we may automatically collect certain information when you access and interact with the Service. This includes information such as:

  • Your Device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages of our Service that you visit
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Time spent on pages
  • Unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data

When you access our Service from a mobile device, we may additionally collect details such as your mobile device type, operating system, and mobile browser information.

How We Use the Collected Data

The Company uses the collected data for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide and improve our Service: We use Usage Data to monitor and analyze how our Service is accessed and used, ensuring a better user experience.
  • Communicate with You: Although we do not require registration, we may occasionally send updates or important notices through the app.
  • Enhance functionality and security: We analyze Usage Data to diagnose issues, enhance the security of our Service, and improve overall functionality.
  • For business analysis: We may use your data for internal research and analysis to understand usage trends and assess the effectiveness of our Service.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share data with third-party companies who help us monitor, analyze, and improve our Service.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law or in response to a valid request by public authorities, we may disclose your data.
  • For business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred as part of the business assets.

Data Retention and Security

We retain Usage Data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Once the data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

Although we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Transfer of Data

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located in different jurisdictions. By using our Service, you consent to such transfers as long as the data is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not intended for anyone under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided data to us, please contact us immediately.

Links to Other Websites

Our Service may contain links to external sites. We have no control over and are not responsible for the content or privacy policies of those sites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
